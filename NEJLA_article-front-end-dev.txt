

WHAT DOES A FRONT-END DEVELOPER DO ?

firstly, front-end developer creates, design websites and applications
using html css and javascript and they determine where to place the elements of the website so it's easy to navigate for the user.

secondly front-end developers earn 88.000$ a year and the demand for this job is growing.
This job can be flexible because you can work on your own, for a company or for a non profit organisation. And you can also work across the country or the globe.

an other thing to remember is that this career increases you creativity and problem-solving skills. However, the skills you want to have as a front-end developer are the 3 languages(html,css,javascript), understanding the frameworks for the languages and understanding how to use different software.
